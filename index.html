USF Biomedical Engineering Society

BMES is a community that is dedicated to serving the needs of biomedical engineering professionals. BMES is your connection to the latest research, bioengineering advances in technology, as well as the latest trends that impact acadeia and industry. You can also attend many of our events where you can connect with peers who share the same professional interests as you.

Our Mission

To help inspire and stimulate all students at the University of South Florida to achieve their full potential as an engineer or leader, improving the quality of life in biomedical engineering related aspects, and promote education and professional development.
